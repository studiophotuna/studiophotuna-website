Interactive Client Screens

Frictionless workflow for guests & admins

From a branded "Tap to Start" trigger to a printed strip and QR gallery hand-off — every guest screen is timed, on-brand, and built to reset itself for the next walk-up.

1 Branded Welcome Trigger

A full-bleed, on-brand "Tap to Start" lobby screen invites guests to launch a session with zero staff intervention.

2 Template Selection

Guests pick a Sample, Classic, or 6×2 layout under a live countdown so the queue keeps moving.

3 Retake Review

Captured shots load instantly with a configurable retake allowance, so guests only redo the frames they want.

4 Strip Assembly Preview

Approved photos are arranged live into the final strip layout side-by-side, exactly as it will print.

5 Tone & Frame Studio

One-tap tone presets — Normal, B&W, Sepia, Vintage, Warm, Cool — plus frame styles update the live strip preview.

6 Gallery Generation

While the strip prints, the system quietly generates the QR code and online gallery in the background.

7 Live Print + QR Hand-off

A scannable QR code appears immediately so guests can download photos and GIFs while the print finishes.

8 Auto-Reset for Next Guest

A thank-you screen confirms the print is ready, then the kiosk resets itself for the next walk-up — no admin needed.

Frequently asked questions

Everything aspiring and active operators ask before subscribing.

No. Studio Photuna runs natively on any Windows 10/11 PC, so you can use a laptop with a tripod and external camera to get started. A kiosk enclosure is optional and only needed if you want a more permanent installation.

Studio Photuna supports most DSLR and mirrorless cameras that offer USB tethering or PC Control mode. Popular Canon EOS and Sony Alpha models work out of the box. The app scans connected USB ports and auto-detects supported devices.

Yes. The output module works with standard dye-sublimation printers. You can configure orientation, margins, cut borders, and the number of copies per session directly in the app settings.

Yes. Operators can enable the retake option in the booth flow settings. Guests will see a preview after capture and can choose to retake before the strip is printed.

The template editor supports 4x6, 6x4, and 2x6 strip layouts. Each layout can be customized with frames, overlays, logos, and branding elements through the built-in drag-and-drop editor.

After each photo session, the app generates a unique QR code displayed on screen. Guests scan it with their phone camera to instantly download their digital photos from the cloud. No app install required on the guest's end.

Yes. New operators get a 14-day free trial with full access to all features — camera integration, template editing, QR cloud sharing, and printer configuration. No credit card required to start.

The core capture, template rendering, and printing features work offline. However, QR cloud sharing, session syncing, and remote dashboard access require an active internet connection at the venue.

Pro subscribers receive automatic update notifications inside the app. Updates include new templates, bug fixes, camera driver improvements, and feature additions. You can install updates at your convenience.

Photos captured locally stay on your PC indefinitely. Cloud-uploaded photos (via QR sharing) are retained for 30 days after the event, then permanently deleted from our servers to protect guest privacy.
