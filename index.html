Frequently asked questions

Do I need a kiosk enclosure?

No. Studio Photuna is designed around a Windows-based booth workflow, so you can start before investing in custom hardware.

Can I create my own templates?

Yes. The workflow supports common layouts, photo slots, frames, branding, guides, and event-specific styling.

Does it include galleries?

Online galleries are available through the gallery add-on, with QR access for guests when enabled.

What operating system does it support?

Studio Photuna is built around a Windows-based booth workflow.

Can I use it for weddings and events?

Yes. It is designed for weddings, birthdays, private events, cafe pop-ups, malls, and brand activations.

Can guests retake photos?

The booth flow includes retake controls so operators can decide how guests move through each session.

Can I configure printing?

Yes. The workflow includes printer, paper size, orientation, copies, and output setup.

Can I book Studio Photuna for an event?

Yes. Event bookings are currently available locally within Metro Manila. Use the Book Event form to send your date, venue, event type, and preferred package.

What is required to reserve an event date?

A 50% reservation fee is required to secure the date. The reservation fee is non-refundable but transferable, subject to booking terms.
