Privacy & Security Framework

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

Studio Photuna is committed to safeguarding personal guest data, photostrip prints, digital metadata, and registered operator metrics securely.

1 On-Event Media Storage Policies

During active photobooth capturing processes, guest images are written locally to the computer's directory. If QR sharing services are explicitly routed, captured photo-strips and individual portrait crops are encrypted and uploaded to our high-speed secure content network.

2 Media Retention Limits

Digital guest photostrips and active downloads are retained in our cloud systems for a maximum period of 30 days following event closure. Once this retention window completes, all copies are permanently and irreversibly purged from our database systems to ensure complete guest confidentiality.

3 How We Use Operator Information

Billing configurations, company names, contact numbers, and profile avatars are safely parsed through secure row-level access parameters inside Supabase database engines. Auto-generated license billing confirmations, booking approvals, and recovery triggers are routed through secure, verified threads at notification@studiophotuna.com.

4 Security Audit Compliance

Our operational framework undergoes weekly vulnerability sweeps. Real-time media feeds do not contain tracking parameters, geolocation cookies, or permanent guest-identifying parameters. Guests retain total control over immediate cloud removal options directly from online download panels.

To request an early data purge or audit compliance checklists, contact our information safety team at support@studiophotuna.com.
