Delete Account

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. This exercises your right to erasure under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). When you confirm, we immediately:

  • Delete your profile, avatar photo, and login credentials
  • Cancel your active subscription/license
  • Delete your booth device registrations and hosted galleries
  • Delete your support tickets and their replies

GCash payment records are kept, but with your name and screenshot removed — this is limited financial/accounting information we're required to retain, not personal profile data. If you need a full copy of your data before deleting, contact dpo@studiophotuna.com first.
