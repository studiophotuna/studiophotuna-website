1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you interact with the site. Cookies are widely used across the internet and are essential for making websites work efficiently and providing a better user experience. Some cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser (session cookies), while others remain on your device for a set period of time (persistent cookies).
